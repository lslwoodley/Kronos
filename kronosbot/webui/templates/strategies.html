Kronos Strategy

How entries, exits, and sizing are decided.

Strategy Logic

1

Trend Filter

Only trade when the closing price is above the 200-period SMA. This keeps the bot aligned with the long-term trend.

2

Breakout Confirmation

Enter only when price closes above the highest close of the previous 19 bars. This confirms momentum.

3

Volume / Volatility Spike

Require volume > 150% of the 20-day average, or an ATR spike when volume is unavailable. This filters low-conviction moves.

4

Kronos Forecast Bias

When a Kronos model is loaded, the next-day close forecast must be bullish. Without a model, this component is neutral.

5

Risk Management

Stop-loss is set at 2 × ATR(14) below the entry price. Position size is fixed at 1 mini lot (10,000 units) for v0.
